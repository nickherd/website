Dear East Middle School students and families,                                    2020-2021 School Year

In the event that a student is contact traced, quarantined or out of school for other health reasons, you may wonder what this means for you as a parent or a student.

Parents: Listed below is a guide for helping you support your child while they are working on classwork from home.

Students: Listed below is a guide for how you can stay current in your studies as you complete your school work from home each day of your absence. This way, when you return, you are ready to join us with the current day’s lesson.

When work is complete, there are three options for you to turn in your school work:

  1. Have your parent/guardian drop your assignment(s) off at the office.
  2. Create a Google Doc in your school Google Drive account and complete the assignment(s). Make sure to include a heading. Then click on the "share" tab to share it with us.
  3. If the above options are not possible, you can turn the assignment(s) in to us when you return to school.

NOTE: Photos of work emailed to us will not be accepted for grading. See options above for submitting assignments properly.
